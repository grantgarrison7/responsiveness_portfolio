About Grant Garrison
Grant Garrison was born on July 17, 1997. He attended Westmoreland High School in Tennessee, where he participated in the honors society, cross country, and track & field. He later went on to study Biochemistry at the University of Tennessee. After this endeavor, he began a Master's degree at Lipscomb University that he is still working to complete.
